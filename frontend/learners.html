Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs):

What is LearnEx?
LearnEx is an educational platform that enables users to learn various courses online, possibly in different subjects or fields. It is an eco-friendly design to make learning accessible and engaging.
What payment methods do LearnEx accept?
LearnEx accepts major credit cards, debit cards and payment gateways such as PayPal(or other specific methods you support)
How does LearnEx work?
Users can browse courses, enroll in the ones they are interested in, and complete lessons at their own pace. First user will get certificate upon completion and from second users onwards, they will get a badge upon completion.
Do I need any prior knowledge to enroll in a course?
Some courses may have prerequisites, which will be listed in the course description. Others are designed for beginners and require no prior knowledge.
Can I access LearnEx courses on mobile devices?
Yes, LearnEx is optimized for mobile devices, and you can access courses on smartphones or tablets through a web browser.
